### Bio

Hi, I’m Bethany LaBelle — licensed massage therapist, certified health coach, and the heart behind Good Karma Collective.

I’ve been practicing massage therapy since 1999, and in that time I’ve had the privilege of working with thousands of clients — helping them recover, restore, and reconnect with how good their bodies can feel. I spent years building and running a thriving wellness practice, and I learned something important along the way: the most powerful thing you can offer someone isn’t just a great massage. It’s being truly seen and cared for.

Good Karma Collective is my fresh start — a space I’ve built from the ground up, with intention. A growing list of services and a vision for something bigger: a real wellness community rooted in warmth, trust, and results.

In addition to massage, I’m a certified Functional Medicine Health & Wellness Coach — which means I can support you not just on the table, but in the everyday habits and choices that shape how you feel long-term.

Whatever brings you here — I’m glad you found us.
